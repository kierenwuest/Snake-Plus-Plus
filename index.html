üêç Game Info:

  • Snakes head is orange and starts in the center.
  • To be played on desktop computer.
  • Use keyboard arrow keys to move.
  • Press Enter to reset the game board.
  • Space will stop the snake.
  • The snake likes to eat coloured dots.
  • Eating a dot will give a score and snakes tail will grow the same length as the score.
  • Red dots score between 1-5 and increase speed by 10px/s.
  • Purple dots appear after score 8, score between 1-7 and increase speed by 10px/s.
  • Yellow dots appear after score 30, score between 1-10 and increase speed by 20px/s.
  • Orange dots appear atfter score 40, score between 1-5 and increase speed by 0px/s.
  • Wild dots appear after score 50, score between 1-10 and decrease speed by 20px/s.
